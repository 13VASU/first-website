Oppo F7 review: ABCD -- All 'Bout Camera & Display


Design:


Be it any phone, if there’s a notch on it then that’s the first thing you’ll notice. It’s no different with the Oppo F7. The glossy back attracts not only a lot of attention but also a lot of fingerprints, that made us wipe the back of the device more often than a person should need to.


Performance:


The chipset that powers the flagship smartphone Oppo F7 is a MediaTek Helio P60, an octa-core chipset fabricated on a 12nm process. This chipset is specifically designed to support AI processing, which this handset does


Camera:


The most emphasized feature of the Oppo F7 is the camera. It comes equipped with an Artificial Intelligence-based selfie camera of 25MP Sony sensor, with an f/2.0 aperture and sensor HDR that is claimed to 'capture the real you'. For beautification, the camera has AI Beauty Technology 2.0.


Battery:


The 3400mAh battery on the Oppo F7 lasts just about a day easily with slightly heavy usage. This involves routine tasks, as well as GPS tracking, watching Netflix and more. For a more moderate user, it can sustain more than a day's work load.


Overall the Phone is good to buy.
